Faq

Is this conference for me?

SCNA welcomes any current or aspiring software developers, entrepreneurs, small business owners, development managers, and technology professionals interested in improving their craft, their community, and themselves. We’d love to have you, no matter how broad your interests and experiences.

Will tickets be available at the door?

Yes; however, the “late” price of $550 will be charged.

I’m going to be a little honest here: as a woman, I’ve always been a little intimidated by the under-representation of my gender in this field.

What! Software development is not some “boys’ club”; we like to think of SCNA as an “everyone club.”

What if I bought tickets, but decide I have something more important to do than attend?

Please notify us by October 15th for a full refund. Any cancellations from October 15th to October 26th will be refunded 50% of the purchase price. Tickets are non-refundable after October 26th. If you wish to transfer your registration to another attendee, please approve your nomination via email prior to November 3rd.

Are beginners welcome?

Of course! We will present you with a world of software knowledge to get you on track.

Does the venue have a dress code?

Please restrict your attire to “business casual,” including jeans and tennis shoes, but no shorts or sandals.

Where will the conference be located?

It'll be in downtown Chicago at the Mid-America Club, located inside the AON Center. You can find an address and map on the venue page.

How many years has SCNA been around?

We've been going strong since 2009. This is our fifth year!

Are there any additional events organized by SCNA that I should know about?

Please view our Events Page.

Where can I park?

The AON Center parking garage will likely be your best bet, although, your choices are not restricted to the AON Center's offerings. AON Center parking rates.

Can I book a room at the AON Center?

The AON Center is unfortunately not a hotel, but there are plenty within walking distance.

I'm at the conference, how can I get help?

All of our volunteers will be wearing name badges with a volunteer label. You can also find us at the registration desk or email us. Volunteers will be present at all SCNA functions and events.
